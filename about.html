About QRTZ Robotics

QRTZ Robotics is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing skills in robotics, engineering, and programming. Our club fosters a collaborative environment where members can experiment, innovate, and develop real-world problem-solving abilities through hands-on projects and competitions.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower students with technical knowledge and practical experience in robotics while fostering a passion for STEM. We strive to create an inclusive and dynamic space where members of all skill levels can learn and contribute.

Club History

QRTZ Robotics was founded in the **2016-2017** school year with the goal of fostering a passion for robotics and engineering among students. Since its inception, we have hosted numerous robotics competitions, especially for new and beginner teams, helping them gain experience and develop their skills. We have also hosted IQ and V5 summer camps, providing hands-on learning opportunities for aspiring young roboticists.
